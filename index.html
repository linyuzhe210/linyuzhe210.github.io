I love games and creating gaming experiences.

Hello, I'm Horizon Lin. I have a bachelor's degree in journalism and worked at Minecraft China (Authorized by Microsoft and represented by NetEase Games) in marketing position for two years. Based on my past experience working with Minecraft China, I became very interested in game-based learning. I love creating games to express my ideas, especially in Minecraft. I hoping for creating immersive experiences for kids all over the world sometime in the future.

I continue to develop myself as a game design polymath. I learned C4D and used scene models and 3D props to render visuals to tell stories.
